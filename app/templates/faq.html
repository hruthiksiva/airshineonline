Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in your travel packages?

Our travel packages typically include accommodation, guided tours, and some meals. Details vary by package, so please check the specific package for more information.

How can I book a trip?

You can book a trip through our website, by calling our customer service, or by visiting one of our partner travel agents.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy varies depending on the package. Generally, cancellations made 30 days before departure are fully refundable. Please refer to our detailed policy on our website for more information.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes, we offer discounts for groups. Please contact our customer service for more details on group rates and available discounts.

Can I customize my travel package?

Yes, we offer customizable travel packages. Please reach out to our travel consultants to tailor a package that suits your needs.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit cards, debit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers.

Do you provide travel insurance?

Travel insurance is not included in our packages, but we highly recommend purchasing travel insurance. We can assist you in finding a suitable travel insurance provider.

What should I do if I have an issue during my trip?

If you encounter any issues during your trip, please contact our 24/7 customer support team for assistance.

Are there any additional fees?

Our packages are designed to be all-inclusive, but some activities and services may incur additional fees. Please refer to the package details for any additional costs.

How can I get more information about a destination?

For more information about a destination, you can visit our website's destination guides or contact our travel experts for personalized advice.
