7 Days Trip to Spain

Join us for an unforgettable week exploring the vibrant culture and stunning landscapes of Spain!

Trip Description

This 7-day journey through Spain will take you from the bustling streets of Madrid to the beautiful beaches of Barcelona. Experience the rich history, delicious cuisine, and vibrant nightlife as you travel through one of Europe's most captivating countries.

Itinerary

Day Location Activities
1 Madrid Arrival, city tour, welcome dinner
2 Madrid Prado Museum, Royal Palace, tapas tour
3 Toledo Day trip to Toledo, sightseeing
4 Seville Travel to Seville, Alcazar, Flamenco show
5 Seville Cathedral of Seville, Plaza de España
6 Barcelona Travel to Barcelona, Sagrada Familia, Gothic Quarter
7 Barcelona Park Güell, beach time, farewell dinner

Instructions to Remember

  • Ensure your passport is valid for at least 6 months from the date of entry.
  • Pack comfortable walking shoes as we will be exploring various sites on foot.
  • Keep a copy of your travel insurance and emergency contact numbers with you at all times.
  • Stay hydrated and protect yourself from the sun, especially during outdoor activities.
  • Respect local customs and traditions.
  • Keep an eye on your belongings, especially in crowded areas.
